4 Days Masai Mara and Lake Nakuru Camping Safari


This safari can be a private safari or a joining budget camping safari where you share the vehicle with other guests and the cost is very reasonable at only USD 500 per person Valid from January to December.


DAY 1 Nairobi - Masai Mara


After breakfast Pick up from your hotel around 8.00am and depart Nairobi to Maasai Mara Game Reserve with a stop over at the great rift valley view point. You arrive masai mara late afternoon and proceed for a game drive after check in. Dinner and overnight at camp.

DAY 2 Masai mara full day


After breakfast Go to the park with picnic lunch for a full day exploring this unique park which really forms the northern part of the famous Serengeti National Park. Meals and overnight at camp.


DAY 3 Masai mara - Lake Nakuru


Pre morning game drive and then after breakfast depart for Lake Nakuru with a stop at Narok for lunch.You are going to arrive Nakuru early evening check in at your hotel outside the park for dinner and overnight.


DAY 4 Day Lake Nakuru - Nairobi


After breakfast proceed for a game drive to Lake Nakuru which is world renowned for its masses of flamingoes and is often referred to as the "Pink Lake". Beside birds, it is possible to see other game like Water Buck, Impala, Rhino,giraffe, lion and Buffalo in the gardens surrounding.
After lunch depart to Nairobi arriving around 4/5pm in the evening.
