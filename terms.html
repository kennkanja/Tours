Payment-Your contract for arrangements and services will become binding on receiving 30% deposit, which is wired to our account in Nairobi. The balance should be received 30 days before commencement of the safari or paid in cash US dollars on arrival.

Cancellation - Within the period of 30 to 8 days before commencement of the safari, a fee of 25% of the total cost of the safari shall apply, Within the period of 7 to 2 days before commencement of the safari, a fee of 50% shall apply, Within 2 days before the commencement of the safari, a fee of 100% of the total cost of the safari
