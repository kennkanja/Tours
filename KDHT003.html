6 Day Lake Manyara, Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater Tanzania Wildlife Safari


DDAY 1: Arusha - Lake Manyara National Park.


After breakfast you will drive to Lake Manyara National Park for a game drive at the park with picnic lunch boxes until late in the afternoon when you will drive your lodge or campsite for dinner and overnight.


DAY 2: Lake Manyara - Serengeti National Park.


Early after breakfast you will drive to Serengeti National Park with picnic lunch boxes. You will have a game drive on route via the Olduvai Gorge until late in the afternoon when you will proceed to your lodge or campsite for dinner & overnight.


DAY 3: Serengeti National Park.


You will have a full day game drive at the Serengeti National Park with picnic lunch boxes until late in the afternoon when you will return to your lodge or campsite for dinner & overnight.


DAY 4: Serengeti - Ngorongoro Crater

After breakfast you will have an early game drive at the park before you drive to the Ngorongoro Crater with picnic lunch boxes.You will have a game drive at the crater until late in the afternoon when you will proceed to your lodge or campsite for dinner & overnight.


DAY 5: Ngorongoro Crater

Early after breakfast you will have a full day game drive at the crater with picnic lunch boxes. Late in the afternoon you will drive to your lodge or campsite for dinner & overnight.


DAY 6: Ngorongoro Crater - Arusha.

Early after breakfast you will have a second full day game drive at the crater with picnic lunch boxes. Late in the afternoon you will drive back to Arusha for overnight.
