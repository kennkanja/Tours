Kenya Camping Safaris, Kenya Budget Joining Camping Safari


Welcome to Kenya, the land of Discovery. Of all the countries of Africa, none can match the sheer range of scenery and landscapes found in Kenya, coupled with a great variety and concentration of wild animals and birds.

This is a land where the world's remaining populations of big cats, big mammals and plains game still roam free in their natural habitats.


Following is our range of sample Camping safaris in Kenya. Other than the sampled tours, we also create tailor-made itineraries to suit individual and /or group interests. If therefore you do not find one that meets your requirement, please send us an e-mail telling us your ideas and we will work together to design your dream safari.

While doing this we advise to give attention to preferred mode of transportation, levels of accommodation, parks of interest, safari duration, group size, budget and activities of interest. All our Road Safaris daily.

Also some of this itineraries can be taken as Joining safaris whereby you share the vehicle with other guests with similar itinerary.
