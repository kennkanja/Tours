10 Days / 9 Nights – Selous Game Reserve – Rufiji – Ruaha National Park – Mikumi National Park


Day 1: Dar Es Salaam


Arrive at the Dar Es Salaam International Airport meet our tour guide and he will take you to the designated hotel in Dar Es Salaam city for late dinner and overnight at the hotel.


Day 2: Dar Es Salaam – Selous Game Reserve


After breakfast, depart for Selous Game Reserve with picnic lunch boxes and arrive late in the afternoon for a leisure dinner and overnight at the Rufiji River Camp or other designated available accommodation.


Day 3: Selous Game Reserve – Rufiji River


After breakfast you will take a boat for a safari along the wonderful Rufiji River, which holds the highest concentration of hippos in Africa and a big number of crocodiles. The boat safari will be accompanied with an afternoon game drive before having dinner and overnight at the Rufiji River Camp.


Day 4: Selous Game Reserve - Rufiji River

After an early breakfast you will take a boat ride down the meandering river to the walking safari starting point. With an armed Ranger / Guide, you will walk through the virgin African bushes marking an unforgettable experience. Get a sauna treat at the hot water natural swimming pool before having a boat ride back to the camp for dinner and overnight at Rufiji River Camp.


Day 5: Selous Game Reserve – Ruaha National Park

After breakfast you will depart for Ruaha National Park for dinner and overnight at Ruaha River Lodge or any other designated accommodation in Ruaha National Park.


Day 6: Ruaha National Park

Have a sunrise game drive and after breakfast leave with picnic lunch for a game drive until sun down when you will return to camp for dinner and overnight at Ruaha River Lodge or any other designated accommodation in Ruaha National Park.


Day 7: Ruaha National Park

After breakfast depart (with lunch boxes) for full day game drive at Ruaha National Park, return to camp late in the afternoon for dinner and overnight at Ruaha River Lodge or any other designated accommodation in Ruaha National Park.


Day 8: Ruaha National Park - Mikumi National Park

After breakfast, depart for a morning game drive before returning to the camp for lunch. Depart for Mikumi National Park for a Sundown game drive at Mikumi, dinner, and overnight at Genesis Motel Mikumi.


Day 9: Mikumi National Park

After breakfast you will depart to discover the beauty of Mikumi National Park for full day with picnic lunch returning late at the motel for a sundown dinner and Overnight at Genesis Motel Mikumi.


Day 10: Mikumi – Dar es Salaam

Sunrise tour of the park and return to the motel for lunch and fly to Dar Es Salaam for preparation to connect with your International flight back home.
