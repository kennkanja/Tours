6 Days Amboseli, Masai Mara and Lake Nakuru Camping Safari


This safari can be a private safari or a joining safari


Day 1: Amboseli Camping

Depart Nairobi in the morning to Amboseli National Park on the foot hill of Mt Kilimanjaro - Africa's highest mountain, arriving in time for lunch. Afternoon game drive which provides chance to see the large herds of Elephants the Game Park also contains vast groups of Zebras, Wildebeests, Giraffes, Buffalos, Hippos and Antelopes. Also to be found in Amboseli are lions and Cheeters. with Mt. Kilimanjaro, Africa highest mountain providing beautiful backdrop against the Amboseli herds of elephants. Dinner and overnight at camp


Day 2: Amboseli National Park


Depart Nairobi at 0730hrs and travel to the Amboseli National reserve at the foot of snowcapped Mt. Kilimanjaro, Africa's highest peak. Arrive in time for lunch. Afternoon game drive is taken. Dinner and overnight at Kimana Camp.


Day 3. Amboseli - Nakuru


Depart Amboseli in the morning Via Nairobi for lunch and drive to the Great Rift Valley viewing sceneries enroute, arriving to the Pink Lake often referred so due to itâ€™s Great masses of Flamingos. Arrival is in the early evening and check in at your hotel for dinner and overnight.


Day 4: Nakuru - Masai Mara


Morning game drive to watch and photograph Birds and wild animals. late morning depart for Masai Mara with a stop enroute for lunch. The Maasai Mara's 1510 square kilometers of open savannah, grass land an eco-system which supports huge numbers of bird and mammal species. Lions are found in abundance throughout the park, as are elephant, giraffe, a variety of gazelle species and zebra. Cheetah and leopard are also regularly seen and if lucky, you may also get to see rhinos. Dinner and overnight at camp


Day 5 Masai Mara


Full day in Masai Mara with morning and afternoon game drives. Optional visit to a local Maasai village at $25 per person or hot air balloon ride at $425 available. Meals and overnight at camp


Day 6: Masai Mara- Nairobi


Morning game drive then return to the campsite for breakfast .Drive back through the Masai town of Narok and stop for some souvenir shopping, before continuing across the plains to Nairobi where the safari ends.
