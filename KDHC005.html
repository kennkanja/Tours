5 Days Masai Mara, lake Nakuru and Lake Naivasha Budget Camping Safari


Day 1: Nairobi-Masai Mara

Depart for Masai Mara, drive through the lush Kikuyu farm land to the edge of the Great Rift Valley. Descend the escarpment via the Loita Plains to Masai Mara Game Reserve in time for lunch. Evening game viewing, dinner and overnight at a camp.


Day 2: Masai Mara

Full day spent viewing and exploring this spectacular park renowned for its large numbers of game such as wildebeests, zebras and its majestic predators. Dinner and overnight at the Camp.
