3 Days Amboseli Flying safari Package

3 Days 2 nights Amboseli Serena Lodge Flying Safari Package.


Amboseli Serena Safari Lodge Secluded within a grove of acacia trees, the lodge looks out over the golden savannah plains, with uninterrupted views of the mountain itself. Close by are a series of emerald green swamps, which are fed by the melting snows of Kilimanjaro. These, the only sources of water in this otherwise starkly beautiful but arid park, attract huge numbers of wallowing elephants, hippos, buffalos and plains game. Meanwhile, from the prehistoric site of Observation Hill, the sunset over Kilimanjaro is magnificent.

Day 01: Nairobi - Amboseli


Arriving in time for lunch, you will have some time to relax before departing on an afternoon game drive in the park. With luck, the cloud cover will lift to reveal the spectacular Mt. Kilimanjaro. Return to the lodge in the early evening in time for dinner and Overnight at Amboseli Serena Lodge (LD).


Day 2


With a full day in the park, your options are wide. Both morning and afternoon game drives are offered (with trips to the famous Mzima Springs Hippo Pools and the Shetani Lava Flows). Sundowners can be taken on safari or at the lodge camp, followed by dinner.


Day 02 : Amboseli


Awaken at dawn and depart on an early morning game drive while the light is just perfect for photographing the magnificent Mt. Kilimanjaro. Return to the lodge for breakfast. This afternoon you may want to take a dip in the pool and do some game-viewing from the terrace. In the late afternoon you will have the opportunity for another major game drive. All meals and overnight at Amboseli Serena Lodge (BLD).


Day 03 : Amboseli - Nairobi


After breakfast you will say goodbye to the bush and transferred to the Airstrip where you will depart with the morning flight back to Nairobi Wilson Airport
